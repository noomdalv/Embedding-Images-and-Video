Visit the village on Sweden

The small municipality of Vellinge in Southern Sweden has 33,000 inhabitants and ambitious aims - to lower energy consumption by 30 percent and save 1 million euros each year. Since 2006 Vellinge has been investing in green building technology and infrastructure solutions on its own initiative. Siemens supplies the all-round package - analysis, consultation and implementation. An example for positive collaboration and the energy turnaround at a grass roots level.
